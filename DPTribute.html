DEAD PREZ

RBG in tha Building!

Dead Prez debuted in 2000 - Stic Man hails from Tallahassee, Florida and M1 is from Brooklyn, New York.

Ever since DP's first album, they encouraged Black women to just be themselves, to wear their natural hair and love the skin they are in.

They celebrate intelligent and powerful women instead of coveting superficial airheads with surgical bodies and no substance.

These Unapologetically Afrakan men are unafraid to speak truth to light - their manhood is not for sale and they bow down to no one.

Dead Prez are so necessary in a world that values profits over people and the promotion of self-hatred and insecurities to fuel those profits.
